------Synopsis------

SSC(Server Side Character) is a special mechanism designed for tModLoader servers that forces players to start with fresh characters when they join.
This means they lose all of their items.Though, it will not affect their actual SinglePlayer character.
SSC are what you need if you wish to prevent people from bringing in items from other servers and/or SinglePlayer and force them to start from scratch.

This mod does not depend on Main.ServerSideCharacter, the playing experience will be as smooth as that in single player mode, and will not affect the net interaction in multi player mode.

Important: Mod depends on AutoSave. You can reconfigure the interface to change it. It is recommended to open it.
           Otherwise, you may lose the recently acquired items.(1 min CD)

------Configuration------

The saved data is a complete PLR file, which is theoretically (practically) compatible with all (normal) mod.
Note: During playing, temporary files will be created in the temp folder of the system.
Save location: [Documents]\My Games\Terraria\tModLoader\SSC\[SteamID]\[name].plr

保存的数据为完整的plr文件,理论(实际)上兼容一切(正常)mod.
Note: 在游玩过程中,会在系统的Temp文件夹创建临时文件.
保存位置: [我的文档]\My Games\Terraria\tModLoader\SSC\[SteamID]\[name].plr

------Feature------

1. The content is basically finalized. If there are no major bugs, there will be no updates.
2. Because of the randomness of the storage, the play time will be reset constantly. It may be solved later, but the priority is low.
